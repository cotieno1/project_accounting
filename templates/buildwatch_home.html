Three-gate compliance lock

No payment certificate is approved unless all three gates are satisfied. This connects site reality to money — the same discipline already used for CEO budget and fund-release gates in financial ops.

Gate 1

Programme gate

Drawings, method statements, material submittals, and permits in place before work proceeds on programme.

Gate 2

Inspection gate

Independent site inspection, hold-point sign-off, test results, and closed non-conformances before valuation.

Gate 3

Compliance gate

Valid professional licence, certificate chain, retention rules, and financial authorisation before payment runs.
