BuildWatch Platform — Terms of Use

By registering on BuildWatch, you agree to the following terms governing your use of the infrastructure integrity platform operated under this service.

1. Accuracy of Information

You confirm that all information provided during registration — including your organisation details, professional registration number, licensing body and expiry date — is accurate and current. Providing false or misleading information constitutes a breach of these terms and may be reported to the relevant regulatory body.

2. Professional Accountability

Every action you take on BuildWatch — including site inspections, compliance flag resolutions, payment certificate approvals and variation sign-offs — is recorded in the immutable audit ledger against your professional registration number and timestamp. You accept personal professional responsibility for all actions performed under your account.

3. Inspection and Certification Standards

You agree to conduct all site inspections and quality assessments in accordance with the applicable standards referenced in the project (NCA, KEBS, KRB, BS, ASTM or equivalent). You will not approve work that does not meet the specified standard. You understand that approving substandard work may constitute professional misconduct.

4. Payment Gate Compliance

Where your role involves the approval of payment certificates, you confirm that no certificate will be raised or approved unless the three-gate compliance lock is satisfied: the programme gate, the inspection gate and the compliance gate. You will not attempt to circumvent this mechanism.

5. Data Use and Confidentiality

Project data, pricing information and personnel records on BuildWatch are confidential to the parties assigned to that project. You agree not to share, export or reproduce any project data outside of authorised channels. Anonymised aggregate data may be used by BuildWatch for platform improvement and national infrastructure analytics.

6. Licence Validity

You are responsible for maintaining a current, valid professional licence for the duration of your participation on projects. BuildWatch will notify you 60 days before your recorded expiry date. Expired licences will result in your account being suspended from inspection and certification functions until renewed.

7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of Kenya. Any disputes arising from use of this platform shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of Kenya.

8. Amendments

BuildWatch reserves the right to amend these terms with 30 days' notice. Continued use of the platform after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the amended terms.
